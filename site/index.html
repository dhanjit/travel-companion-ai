What it does

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Knows your travelers

Add everyone going — a strict-veg grandma, a kid who gets carsick, a photographer chasing light. It plans around these specific people, not a generic tourist.

🗺️

Plans the whole drive

Origin, destination, dates → a day-by-day driving plan: departure times, breaks on the right rhythm, meals that fit every diet, sights matched to interests, and where to stay each night.

💬

Adjusts on the fly

“Make day 2 relaxed.” “We're running late.” “Rain — move day 3 indoors.” The plan re-renders instantly. All by car, always.

Guided, context-aware choices

Most planners hand you a blank chat box. This one offers the next decision — and each choice narrows toward a concrete change.

The options aren't a fixed menu. What it offers shifts with who's travelling and the moment — the weather in that window, the time of day, how tired everyone is. Rain rolls in and the covered options move up; the kid's fading and the ambitious plans step back.

You can shape a whole day, or tap “what next here?” on any single stop.
